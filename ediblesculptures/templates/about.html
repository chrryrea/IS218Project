About Us

We are a team of five passionate NJIT students who came together to bring this website to life as part of our IS218 class project. Our goal was to create a user-friendly platform where unique fruit and vegetable sculptures could be showcased and sold. Each member contributed their own skills—from design and development to content and creativity—to build a site that blends functionality with fresh, artistic flair. This project reflects our teamwork, dedication, and excitement for combining technology with edible art.
